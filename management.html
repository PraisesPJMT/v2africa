Board Chairman

Mr. Walter Ahrey

Mr. Walter Ahrey is the Business Development Director and founder of the International Financial Infrastructure Ltd, a company involved with providing solutions and management consulting services for strategy, public financial management, information technology, payment systems, financial inclusion and development finance.

A former Director of Strategy and Performance of the Central Bank of Nigeria, he had worked largely as an Information Technology expert in the Nigerian Ports Authority, Hamburg Port Consulting, Centoria Management Consultancy, Chase Merchant Bank and Bank of the North. He has worked on projects sponsored by the World Bank, International Monetary Fund and International Fund for Agriculture Development (IFAD).

He has provided consulting services for the Office of the Head of Service of the Federation, Federal Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, International Fund for Agriculture Development, National Association of Microfinance Banks and the Association of Non-bank Financial Institutions of Nigeria.

Mr Ahrey has performed lead roles in national projects such as the Project EAGLES of CBN, Financial System Strategy 2020, Payment System Vision 2020, Government Integrated Financial Management Information System and the National Payments Gateway. He is involved with the development of the National Agriculture Resource Centre, Agriculture Market Information System, National Cooperatives Database and National Agriculture Extension Services Network.

Board Member

Prof. Teresa Mwuese Nmadu

Peter Ter is an entrepreneur and a business development provider. He is the founder of Mellifera Foods Limited, a company that implements agricultural value chains and specializes in the manufacture and production of raw materials for foods, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals industries.

Mellifera Foods Limited is currently implementing an apicultural value chain where it produces raw honey and beeswax which is sold as raw materials to other industries.

Prior to founding Mellifera Foods Limited, he founded Ura Systems Limited, an ICT company that was into the sales and servicing of ICT products. Ura Systems Limited provided services to Ministries, Departments and Agencies of government. They also had retainership and provided services to conglomerates and other companies in the private sector.

Peter Ter is the lead business innovator and facilitates programs at V2 Africa Labs.

Board Chairman

Mr. Nkechi Ule

Nkechi Ule is a book entrepreneur and founder of Beacon Bookshops. Beacon Bookshops specializes in the provision of rare literature. She is known for her ability to supply any book at short notice. As a book entrepreneur, she goes beyond selling books and offers books consultancy services, giving advice and recommendations on appropriate books for different age grades.

Her love for books precedes her establishment of Beacon Bookshops. She started her career in the books industry with Life Challenge Africa and later moved to Oasis Distributions Limited. While working she helped customers and the Book stores in the selection and mechanization of books. She also specialized in branding and marketing of extraordinary books. She branded and promoted the book “Purpose Driven Life” in Nigeria while working and consulting for Oasis Distributions Limited. “Purpose Driven Life” sold millions of copies in Nigeria due to her branding and marketing capability. She also consulted and helped set up planning and training programs for the development of staff of Bookstores in the country.

Recently she has been a consultant to authors, helping in the planning and promotion of their books. Her aspiration is to be a publisher helping young authors take up the act of book writing as a profession and a business.

Prior to her advent to the book business, she worked with The Navigators, a Christian organization. During her stay with The Navigators, her responsibility included influencing and helping young ladies make right choices and develop lifelong convictions. She also helped them stay focused on their life goals.

She brings a wealth of skills to the board from her experience as a book consultant, entrepreneur and life coach. She is also a skilled facilitator.
