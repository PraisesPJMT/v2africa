V2 Africa Labs is a business development organization that empowers entrepreneurs to build successful and sustainable businesses. We offer a range of programs, mentorship, and funding opportunities to help entrepreneurs grow their businesses and create positive social impact.

We inspire entrepreneurs to live out their gifting in business by building their capacity to flourish and have a positive impact on society.

To inspire entrepreneurs/business founders to success and make a positive impact on their communities.
